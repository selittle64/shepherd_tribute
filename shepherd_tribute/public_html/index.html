Sam Shepard: American Myth

"I feel like I've never had a home, you know? I feel related to the country, to this country, and yet I don't know exactly where I fit in... There's always this kind of nostalgia for a place, a place where you can reckon with yourself."

The Life and Times of an American Maverick

  • Born: November 5, 1943. Fort Sheridan, Illinois.
  • 1961: Graduated from Duarte High School, Duarte, California.
  • 1964: Cowboys published.
  • 1978: Buried Child wins Pulitzer prize for Drama.
  • 1978: Stars in Terrence Malick's Days of Heaven.
  • 1983: Plays Chuck Yeager in film adaptation of The Right Stuff, nominated for Academy Award
  • 1986: A Lie of the Mind wins Drama Desk Award and NY Drama Critic's Circle Award.
  • 2004: The God of Hell published.
  • Died: July 27, 2017.
